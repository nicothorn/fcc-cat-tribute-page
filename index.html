Fun facts about Felis catus:

  • Cats domesticated themselves.
  • Cats sleep around 16 hours a day, which means they spend approximately 65% of their lives sleeping.
  • If cats could text you back, they probably wouldn't.
  • The oldest known cat to have ever lived was named Creme Puff and he lived for 38 years and 3 days.
  • Cats have built-in sensors that know when you're trying to get work done, that alerts them that it's time to sleep on your keyboard.
  • Many cats enjoy having pet humans who do their bidding.
  • Cats have whiskers on the back of their front legs.
  • Cats are a liquid, as liquids take the shape of the container while maintaining a constant volume. Ergo, cats are liquid.
  • Cats are ungovernable.
  • Cats walk like camels and giraffes. These three animals are the only animals that walk so they move both their right feet, then both their left feet.
  • Cats are objectively the best creatures to grace the planet, and they know it.
